# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment
We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and constructive community.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and professional.
- Give constructive feedback.
- Assume good intent.
- Keep discussions relevant and useful.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment or hate speech
- Personal attacks
- Spam or deceptive promotion
- Sharing private data without consent

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove content that violates this policy.
